Newark - - - Delaware

I grew up in Newark, Delaware, which is a small city in the second smallest state in the country. Though small, it does have a downtown area known as Main Street with shops and restaurants. This is primarily due to it being a college town as it is home to the University of Delaware (UD). Growing up, my family would spend summers at the UD pool and I also took ice skating lessions at the ice rink. Some notable people that attended the University are Joe Flacco, Johnny Weir, and President Joe Biden.
